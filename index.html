about me.

My name is Nate Ober and I manage, build and implement large scale software systems.

Working for the Utah State Office of Education, I am the project manager and system administrator for UTIPS Core and last generation UTIPS, both online formative assessment systems. Over the last year, these two services served up more than 6 million tests and were used by teachers to give them immediate feedback regarding their students’ abilities.

I am also the technology project manager for summative assessments in Utah, responsible for developing technical requirements, troubleshooting large scale technology problems and acting as the primary technical contact for all CRT computer-based testing in Utah. In this regard, I have experience writing RFPs, participating in regular stand-ups with developers, and coordinating action between districts, contractors and the USOE.

I have an extensive history developing applications in PHP, C# and Perl. I have more than 10 years experience working with web based SaaS applications at every level from hardware and networking to system administration, interface design and programming.

I am passionate about technology in education and the future of technology in our schools.
