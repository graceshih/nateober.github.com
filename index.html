about me.

I am a product manager, tinkerer and system administrator.

Working for the Utah State Office of Education as an Education Specialist, I am the project manager and system administrator for UTIPS Core and last generation UTIPS, both online formative assessment systems. Over the last year, these two services served up more than 6 million tests and were used by teachers to give them immediate feedback regarding their students’ abilities.

I am also the technology project manager for summative assessments in Utah, responsible for developing technical requirements, troubleshooting large scale technology problems and acting as the primary technical contact for all CRT computer-based testing in Utah. In this regard, I have experience writing RFPs, participating in regular stand-ups with developers, and coordinating action between districts, contractors and the USOE.

I started a web development company in the 90s and have a history developing applications in PHP, .Net and Perl. I have more than 10 years experience working with web based SaaS applications at every level from hardware and networking to system administration, interface design and programming.

I am passionate about technology in education and the future of technology in our schools.

To find out a little bit more about my recent experience you can visit my LinkedIn page. If you would like to follow me on Twitter or Google Plus, you can find me at nateober.com/twitter or nateober.com/googleplus.
